Discovery

At Floodline, we've split discovery into its own phase to ensure a thorough understanding of your project needs.

With many software vendors, the project discovery process tied to a large project commitment. We understand that within a startup, those types of commitments can be challenging. By offering Discovery as a standalone service, we allow you to explore your ideas without the pressure of a full project commitment, leaving you with an actionable Solution Plan to inform your scope and decisions moving into development.

Whether you build with Floodline or take the plan elsewhere, Discovery gives you the clarity to move forward with confidence.

  • A clear Solution Plan outlining what to build and why
  • A refined, MVP-ready scope with prioritized features
  • A realistic timeline and cost estimate you can plan around

Development Support

We offer a wide range of development support services to help you build and scale your product effectively.

From Fractional CTO services to flat-rate projects to hourly development, we tailor our support to meet your specific needs.

  • Technical architecture and design
